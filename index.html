Quote Builder

Fill in the details below, then generate a webpage to copy/paste into an email. The Dargie logo and brand styling are built in — photos are embedded automatically and videos play from Vimeo links, so the file stays small.

01 Cover

This becomes the opening screen of the presentation. The quote date is stamped automatically when you generate.

📅

02 Acts

Each act showcases one offering. Add as many photos as you like (viewers click through them), and optionally a Vimeo video — if an act has both, the video floats over the corner of the photos. Photos only, video only, or both all work; the layout adapts automatically.

03 Quote

Line descriptions come from your act headings automatically — just add the number of performers and unit price for each. Use the button below for extra lines not tied to an act (e.g. travel). All prices are exclusive of GST ("+ GST" is added automatically), and the subtotal adds the unit prices only.
